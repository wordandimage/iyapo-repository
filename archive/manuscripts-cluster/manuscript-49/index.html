Name

Rachel Jamison

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Self Defense

Domain:Game

“The Cube” Families in the dystopia are on a never ending quest to unlock the cube; wrong combination can be deadly so few people try. Similar to Excalibur, the Sword of King Arthur associated with magical powers. Only one person every hundred years can solve it. Solver of puzzle given power (either physical or intellectual) to change the world. No guarantee of whether this person is good or evil.
