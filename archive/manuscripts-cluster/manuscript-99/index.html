Description

An Apple Watch of other kind of bracelet that citizens are required (plugin) to wear by the government. The bracelet can be used as a form of identification for things like a driver's license or voting. But the bracelet would also be used to display if someone is registered as a sex offender and would notify others around them via vibrations. If your band is purple, that might mean you're a sex offender or other colours for other "serious crimes." Most people would want to wear the band/be happy to wear it bc it makes registration convenient.
