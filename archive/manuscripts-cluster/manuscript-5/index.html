#0005

Name

Teresa Lamb

Narrative:Apocalyptic

Object:Makes Sound

Domain:Politics

During the apocalypse, nobody can leave their shelters so people created a way to show their patriotism by creating a daily regime of placing a hand over their hearts to hear the”pledge of Allegiance” as it evolves in the new state of the world. Politicians of the world would broadcast a new pledge everyday to inform the public about the universal values to which they should abide.
