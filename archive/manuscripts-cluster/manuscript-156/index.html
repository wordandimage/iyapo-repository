Sophia Williams

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Has a Motor

Domain:Music

I am thinking about a protective shield.  Even a bubble.  Music has a great way of creating space and transferring spaces.  Especially music with intention.  I am wondering about how to make music a literal form of protection.  A few ideas come to mind.  Glasses, antidepressants, and a literal bubble.  Glasses -  would love to see what I’m hearing.Antidepressant  - would love the music to heal from the inside out Bubble - would love for music to wrap me up and protect me from or just wrap me up and hold me.
