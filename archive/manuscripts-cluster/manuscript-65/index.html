Name

Freedom of Movement. Thought and Speech Suit

Narrative:Apocalyptic

Object:Wearable

Domain:Security

This artifact enables the wearer to move undetected by the police state. Its lining, when activated, shields the wearer from detection and allows the wearer to communicate with others unmonitored. Ot also protects and deflects the wearer form bullets, harmfull rays, and device hacks. It includes untraceable sedative darts.
