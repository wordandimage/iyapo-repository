#0047

Name

Amani Sampson

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Has a Motor

Domain:Game

WIND GHOSTS. Large fans blow wind in a dark room. From the fans, stips of fabric blow. This is a game you play with your body. You enter the room and try to play hopscotch. If you miss a spot, lose your balance -- you must start again. The places of the hopscotch squares change every time you play the game/have to restart. You win the game when you make it across the room without losing your sense of self or rooting. This is about forgetting and remembering. You are reminded of ghosts.
