My drawing is a train station inside a snow globe.  In the train track there’s projected signs of danger all around.  The globe shows a destructive future where the government went more overboard and watches everything.  Everybody would wear the same outfit with a necklace that detects movement and moods.  It also translates what you are thinking in different languages.
