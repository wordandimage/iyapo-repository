#0038

Name

Amira Moore

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Changes Color

Domain:Fashion

The state of the city is such that overcrowding has perpetuated rampant homelessness. Most people don’t have traditional homes. Instead people live in their clothing. Infused with rigid fibres that lengthen when attached to a power source. The power source is located in the black muffler and is also known as the ankh. This model is configured to blend into the rocky landscape of city rubble.
