Name

M.P

Sensory suit that simulates the feeling of being underwater.This suit also has sensory units inside that collect data from subjects such as heat, pressure, vital signs etc to evaluate the subject during their “underwater experience”.This suit helps trauma victims and other people with water related phobias as a form of therapy. Also useful for Alzheimer's patients who aren’t capable of swimming to have a satisfying feeling to the their need.
