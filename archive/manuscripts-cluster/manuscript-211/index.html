Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Transmits Data

Domain:Environment

This is a headset that runs on solar power and is made up of broken parts of different things - scrap metal, spare parts, anything that’ll work.  It senses brain waves to detect emotions, and when you're stressed or angry it plays music to calm you.  This works great with kids with autism or a mental disorder.  It’s made out of light weight things so it almost feels like feathers.
