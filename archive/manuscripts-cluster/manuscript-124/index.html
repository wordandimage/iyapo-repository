Narrative:Dystopian

A device that allows the wearer to know the inner emotion.  A true intention of people speaking around them.  Can identify key phrases and give background information or contextual stories.  Filters sounds and gives information about the speaker and about the things people say.  Can identify emotions in the voice and display the data on the veil, or on a display screen.  Can be used with or without the veils depending on the hostility of the region.
