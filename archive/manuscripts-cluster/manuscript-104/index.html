#0104

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Changes Color

Domain:Education

In a dystopian world this artifact can analyze and determine whether something is ok for the planet or not. It changes colors when something is good or bad for the earth. It can also teach someone who opens the artifact anything they want to know about our planet and what choices we can make to see a change in a dystopian world.
