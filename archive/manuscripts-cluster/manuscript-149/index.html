#0149

Name

Ara Kling

Narrative:Revolutionary

Object:Pumps Air

Domain:Food

A clear heart shaped pod that can be easily transported to any location in the world. The heart pod has a cap for water and a cord to pump air into it.  Once you add a single drop of water and air pressure (from a few puffs of air to a direct line) the seed like object will transform into a full nutritious meal.  Then someone can tear open the bag or pod to eat a meal of their choosing.  This way no one will ever be hungry.
