#0173

Name

Semaj Bishop

Narrative:Apocalyptic

Object:Makes Sound

Domain:Education

A machine that allows one to learn more about themselves.  The person puts their hand inside the machine and gives their birthday, sign and address, home number  and brief description of who they think they are.  The machine analyzes them and tells them things they may not know about themselves. Then it gives suggestions on who you should meet because you are like them and it shows who is not like you and the location who is furthest and closest to your location.  It speaks to you.  Also if you want to meet the recommended person it contacts them.  It also reads your expression to tell if you are lying and to read yourself clearly.
