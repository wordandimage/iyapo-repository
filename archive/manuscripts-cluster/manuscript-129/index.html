Device attaches to ear, plays stories + glows when your story is being transmitted.The first step in healing racial tension is through the promotion of understanding.  This device works by allowing people to listen to the stories of strangers in their area.  Users are prompted to take walks in unfamiliar places.  As they proceed, the device will both pick up and play you the stories of strangers and play your own story to them so by the end of the exchange you will feel more connected to those around you regardless of age, race or gender.
