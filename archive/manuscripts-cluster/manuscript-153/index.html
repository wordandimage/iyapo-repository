Name

Melissa Edmond

Narrative:Apocalyptic

Object:Has a Motor

Domain:Food

The Afro-Tent is the motorized cart carrying everything in the post-apocalyptic world.  The tent will have everything a child of the sun needs.  The Afro-Cart will never forget you when the world is gone.  The cart travels from one dystopian town to the next providing everything a person may need to survive from food, to clothes, news and hair products, artifacts and trinkets to remind one of the past, a rich history of Africa where human life began and spread throughout the world.  The car is solar powered and goods are exchanged through bargaining.
