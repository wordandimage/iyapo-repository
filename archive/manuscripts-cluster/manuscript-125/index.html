#0125

Name

David Gordon

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Self Defense

Domain:Security

Mandatory hollowsuit all citizens must wear.  Depending on status, societal role, criminal records, the suit is filled to varying degrees with different materials.  Criminals undesirables, etc. have their suite filled to the brim with wet corn starch, to limit movement.  The neo upper class fill their suits with oil as a symbol of wealth, like a ROLEX or MERCEDESCalvin Klein - ‘Crude’
